Open Knowledge Project

Curated Zettels.

Zettelkasten is a lifelong knowledge project and personalized tool for optimizing thinking and writing; it is composed of individual notes, or zettels, that are linked together through a system of hypertext. Over time, clusters of hyperlinked zettels naturally connect and create a web of easily surfable knowledge. Here, you will find a few of my selected zettels; however, the full collection can be found here.
