Lifelong Learning

My Education.

I graduated Cum Laude with a B.S. in Chemical Engineering from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. The time I spent at my alma mater represents a period of constant trial and growth, and I loved every minute of it. Studying engineering transformed the way I think about and interact with the world; it made me an adept problem solver, confident to tackle any of life's challenges.
